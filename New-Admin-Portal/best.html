About Kanyadet School

Leading the way in Competency-Based Curriculum education since 1974

Our Mission

To provide quality CBC education that develops competent, confident, and creative learners who are ready to excel in Kenya's education system and beyond.

We are fully aligned with Kenya's 2-6-3-3-3 education system, offering comprehensive education from Grade 1 through Grade 8, preparing our students for junior secondary school and future success.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's premier primary and junior school, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and full implementation of the CBC curriculum.

Our state-of-the-art facilities in Nairobi, experienced TSC-registered teachers, and commitment to CBC values ensure every learner develops the seven core competencies required by KICD.

Admissions Information

Join Kenya's premier CBC school for Term 1, 2025

Admission Requirements

  • Duly filled admission form
  • Original birth certificate and 2 photocopies
  • Immunization card (for PP1 & PP2)
  • Previous school leaving certificate & report forms
  • Copy of parents'/guardians' ID cards
  • Two recent passport-size photos
  • Admission assessment for Grade 4 and above
  • Medical examination report from registered clinic

2025 Fee Structure

Term 1-3 Fees KSh 45,000
Admission Fee (One-time) KSh 15,000
Lunch Program (per term) KSh 12,000
Transport (per term) KSh 18,000
Total (Full Package) KSh 90,000
