Frequently Asked Questions

Is my student data secure? ▼

Absolutely! We use 256-bit SSL encryption, regular security audits, and comply with international data protection regulations. Your data is backed up daily and stored in secure data centers.

Can I export my data anytime? ▼

Yes! You can export student data to PDF, Excel, or CSV formats at any time. You completely own your data and can leave whenever you want.

How long does it take to set up? ▼

You can be up and running in minutes! Sign up, import your data, and start using StudPortal. Our team can help with bulk imports if needed.

What support do you offer? ▼

We offer email support for all plans, priority support for Professional plans, and 24/7 support for Enterprise customers. We also provide video tutorials and comprehensive documentation.

Is there a free trial? ▼

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional plan. No credit card required. Test all features risk-free and see if StudPortal is right for you.
